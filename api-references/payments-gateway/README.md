# Payments Gateway

Inyo gateway allows clients to perform **pull** and **push** operations, enabling integration with several different providers such as card schemes and banks.

Supporting a wide range of payment methods, Inyo gateway offers:

* **Credit/Debit Cards**: Accept payments through major card networks.
* **ACH (Automated Clearing House)**: Electronic bank transfers in the United States.
* **3DS (Three-Domain Secure)**: Secure card payments with Cardinal.
* **Built-in smart-routing and antifraud**: flexibility that makes payments robust and more secure.

Designed to be scalable and secure, Inyo gateway is ideal for meeting the needs of businesses seeking a fast and reliable payment solution. In the following chapters, we will explore how to configure, integrate, and operate the gateway.
